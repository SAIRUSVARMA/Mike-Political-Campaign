About

Mike Ramaglia brings nearly 30 years of law enforcement expertise to his role as the newly elected Sheriff of Passaic County.

About Me

MIKE RAMAGLIA

As a proud family man raising his children in Passaic County, Sheriff Adamo combines his professional experience with a deep personal investment in the well-being of Passaic County. His leadership focuses on creating safer neighborhoods and a stronger, united county for all residents.​

A lifelong advocate for community safety, he is committed to fostering trust, accountability, and innovation in public service.

A lifelong resident of Passaic County, and a ​member of the Passaic County Sheriff's Office since the age of 19 serving practically every rank, Sheriff Adamo has deep roots in Passaic County.

Our Goals

Empowering communities through innovation, inclusivity, and sustainable progress to build a brighter future for all.

Ready to work on day one

to deliver results, drive meaningful change, and create a future full of opportunities for all.

Improve our roads and bridges

Enhance road safety and connectivity by upgrading aging infrastructure, modernizing bridges, and ensuring efficient, long-lasting transportation networks.

Make our country safer place to live

Strengthen public safety by enhancing law enforcement resources, modernizing emergency response systems, promoting community policing, and addressing the root causes of crime to create a secure and peaceful environment for everyone.
